1 Staff & Shift
Shift management and Blocked time are now fully developed. The Staff & shift menu is complete — every sub-tab and every action works end-to-end.
Capabilities:
Shift management — Create, edit, view, archive, and assign shifts. Click any shift row to open its full detail. The "+ Add" button now wires through to a complete shift creation flow.
Blocked time — Create and edit blocked time slots so each staff member's unavailable hours are accurately reflected across the schedule.
Sub-tab navigation — Staff list, Shift management, and Blocked time all live in the same Staff & shift module with a single header. Switching between them keeps your search and filter state.

2 Pay rate
Simplified the pay rate setup for the Monthly salary type — same cleanup pattern we already had on Flat rate.
Capabilities:
No "Additional settings" on Monthly salary — When you pick "Monthly salary" as the pay rate type, the attendance toggles ("Only count checked-in customers" and "Include late-cancelled customers") no longer appear. Monthly pays a fixed amount regardless of who attended, so the toggles aren't relevant. The pay rate detail page also drops the Additional settings tab for monthly rates.

General updates
"POS" is now "Point of Sale" — Renamed every "POS" label across the admin (sidebar, page header, global search, schedule popup, reports, role permissions, confirmation modals, dashboard activity feed) to the full "Point of Sale" name. No functional change — purely a clarity update for client-facing copy.
Customer self-bookings reach admin instantly — When customers book a class on their side, the new booking appears immediately in the admin's customer profile, dashboard, and class roster — and a "New booking" notification pops up. No refresh needed.

1 Staff & shift
Manage staff, their shifts, and time off across every location — Shifts have a List and a Week calendar, and Time off has a List and a Month calendar.
Capabilities:
Manage shifts from the Week calendar — Every staff row in the Week view has a three-dot menu to assign a shift, unassign a shift, and — for instructors — jump straight to their schedule. Everything happens without leaving the calendar.
One staff member, many shifts — A staff member can now hold more than one shift. Assigning a shift adds it alongside their existing ones instead of replacing them — the same whether you assign from the Week calendar or from a shift's own page.
Shifts stay in the right location — When assigning a shift, the picker only shows shifts that belong to that staff member's location, so a South staffer can't be placed on a shift from another branch.
No double-booking — Assigning a shift that clashes with one the staff member already has on the same day and time is blocked with a clear message. Back-to-back shifts, where one ends exactly as the next begins, are allowed.
Pick which shift to remove — Unassigning shows the staff member's assigned shifts so you can remove just one, or clear them all at once. The List view asks for confirmation before removing.
Week calendar matches the schedule — The Week view now uses the same day header and shift cards as the class Schedule, and the date row stays pinned to the top as you scroll.
Time off Month calendar — Time off has a new Month view that mirrors the class schedule's month layout, colour-coded by reason — sick, vacation, training, or other. Click any entry to open it for editing, and pressing Back returns you to the Time off tab.

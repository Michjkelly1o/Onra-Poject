Note:
This push is a mix of two things: a new sales-commission flow for how staff get paid, and a round of polish across freezing, marketing, and the schedule. Today's feedback from the meeting is not tackled yet in this push — we only ship what was completed this week and the previous week. In the prototype you may still see some 404 pages; that means the page/module/feature is still being developed.
New side for admin: to navigate to admin please change the link to "https://onra-poject.vercel.app/admin/dashboard" and for the instructor side, please change it back to "https://onra-poject.vercel.app/instructor/dashboard"
Interactive experience — This is a fully interactive prototype. Feel free to click, create, edit, and delete items to test the workflows.
Live updates — The prototype uses realistic sample data. Any changes you make apply live as you navigate between different screens.
Session persistence — Any data you create, edit, or cancel survives a page refresh and tab close. Your demo session sticks until you explicitly reset.

1 Compensation & Payroll
How the studio pays its team — pay rates, sales commission, and payroll runs.
Capabilities:
Commission & Bonus On Monthly Rates — A Monthly pay rate can now carry sales commission (a percentage or a fixed amount per membership, package, or gift card sold) plus a bonus that kicks in once the person passes a monthly sales target.
Commission On The Payout Detail — Each person's payout page now shows a clear breakdown of the commission and bonus they earned for the period, next to their pay.
Payroll Covers Every Role — The payroll list and the Run payroll flow now include all staff, not just instructors — anyone on a salary or earning commission shows up.
Real Monthly Totals — A staff member on a monthly salary now shows their actual "Total earnings this month" (salary plus commission) instead of a blank AED 0.
Tidier Payout Summary — The little earnings box on the payout page is cleaner — the class counter is removed for salaried staff, and the page is now titled "Staff details" since it covers every role.
Moved To The Right Place — The sales-commission summary now lives on the payout detail where pay is reviewed, rather than on the staff profile.

2 Point of Sale
The checkout screen for selling memberships, packages, and gift cards.
Capabilities:
Credited-to Staff Picker — Checkout now asks who each sale is credited to. That attribution is what drives a staff member's sales commission, so it's a required choice before completing the sale.

3 Marketing
Announcements and campaigns sent to members.
Capabilities:
Pick The Event To Promote — On an Event campaign using "Book an event", you now choose exactly which upcoming session the button links to, from a single-select list.
Pick The New Class To Promote — On a New Class campaign, you choose one class launching within the next 7 days.
Searchable Pickers — Both selectors have a search box, so a long list of sessions is quick to narrow down.

4 Schedule
The studio calendar of classes, private sessions, and recovery.
Capabilities:
Simpler Type Filter — The schedule's type filter now reads plainly as Classes, Private, and Recovery.

5 Services
Creating a bookable service — a 1:1 private session or a recovery & wellness session.
Capabilities:
Separate Create Menus — Making a Private session and making a Recovery & wellness session are now two distinct flows. The Private menu creates a private session, the Recovery menu creates a recovery session — no extra type picker to fill in, since the menu already decides it.

6 Customer
Member profiles and their activity across the studio.
Capabilities:
Class-count Badges Only — On a member's class, private, or recovery detail, the badges now show only how many times they've attended (1st, 2nd, 3rd… and up, with no cap). The birthday and new-member pills are removed.
Member-side Freeze — Members can now freeze their own membership, controlled by the studio's freeze policy.
Freeze Reasons — Freezing a membership now captures a reason, both when a member does it and when a staff member does it.

7 Settings
Studio configuration.
Capabilities:
Freeze Policy — A new Freeze policy area lets each studio turn membership freezes on or off and manage the list of reasons members can choose from.
Cancellation Reasons — The list of cancellation reasons is now managed inside Booking rules → Cancellation policy, and feeds both the admin and member cancel-plan screens.
Portal Preferences As A Panel — "Portal preferences" in Branding now opens as a step-by-step side panel, matching the Design settings experience.

General updates
Consistent Chart Hover Labels — Every dashboard and insights chart now reads money the same way on hover — clean "AED 15,000" figures, with the stray "(AED)" labels removed.
Everything Stays In Sync — Changes flow correctly across all the updated screens: a sale, a freeze, or a pay-rate edit reflects everywhere it should in the same session.

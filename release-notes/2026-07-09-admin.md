Interactive experience — This is a fully interactive prototype. Feel free to click, create, edit, and delete items to test the workflows.
Live updates — The prototype uses realistic sample data. Any changes you make apply live as you navigate between different screens.
Session persistence — Any data you create, edit, or cancel survives a page refresh and tab close. Your demo session sticks until you explicitly reset.

1 Referral
The full referral program — who earns what, when the reward unlocks, and how the program is performing.
Capabilities:
Overview and Setup tabs — Overview shows program status, monthly budget progress, top referrers, and quick KPIs. Setup keeps the configuration (rewards, eligibility, member-facing copy).
Two reward types — A referral reward can now be a Class Credit OR an Account Credit in your studio currency (AED). The Amount field switches its unit to "credit(s)" or "AED" based on the choice.
Simplified triggers — The reward-release trigger reads simply as "Sign up", "First purchase", or "First class" — no long explanations under each option.
Cleaner "Eligibility & safeguards" section — Renamed from "fraud controls" and every rule reworded to be easier to read.
Top referrers list — Full paginated list of referring members. Click any row to open that member's profile directly.

2 Customers
The member profile and everything about their plans, payments, and now account credit.
Capabilities:
Wallet tab — New tab on the member profile that holds their account credit balance (AED). Shows a running ledger of every credit and debit with reason, source, amount, and date.
Live balance — The balance is calculated from the ledger — no separate stored number that can drift.
Pinned pagination on Wallet — Pagination sits fixed at the bottom of the tab, matching the Plan tab.
Consistent Credit-left bar — In the Plan tab the "Credit left" bar is the same size on every row, whether the label reads "Unlimited" or "0/10".
Cancelled plans styled like Expired — Cancelled plan rows now grey out with the same treatment as Expired ones.

3 Pay rate & Payroll
Sales commission is now real — the system tracks recorded sales and shows earnings on the staff member's own profile.
Capabilities:
Sales credited automatically — Every POS sale is credited to whoever is logged in when the sale closes. No manual "sold by" step needed.
Commission on the staff profile — Non-instructor staff on a Monthly pay rate see a Sales commission card on their profile — packages and memberships broken down as "net sales × %" so any number can be traced back to the actual sales.
Refund clawback — Refunding a sale in the same period reduces that salesperson's commission automatically.
Instructor pay rates stay class-based — Instructors can only be assigned pay rates that carry no sales commission. The pay-rate dropdown hides Monthly rates that have commission set, so you can never assign one by accident.

4 Customer Notifications
Tightened the copy in the Delivery hours side panel — same behaviour, less wordy.
Capabilities:
Cleaner section header — "Set quiet hours" replaces the longer "Set a quiet window so members aren't messaged at unsociable hours."
Fewer explanations — Redundant explanations under the toggles were removed. What stays is what matters.

General updates
Codebase tidy-up — Cleared out legacy screens and duplicate data sources so the platform is leaner. Every module you interact with is identical to before, but the app is faster to load and cleaner to maintain going forward — important when we move to a real database later.

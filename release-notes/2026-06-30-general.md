Interactive experience — This is a fully interactive prototype. Feel free to click, create, edit, and delete items to test the workflows.
Live updates — The prototype uses realistic sample data. Any changes you make apply live as you navigate between different screens.
Session persistence — Any data you create, edit, or cancel survives a page refresh and tab close. Your demo session sticks until you explicitly reset.

1 Tax
The settings screen where the owner controls how tax is applied across the studio. Still being polished — will appear in the next demo push.
Capabilities:
VAT and Income tabs — One unified list for every tax rate, separated into a VAT view and an Income tax view so the owner sees only what's relevant.
Three rate types — Default, Zero-rated, and Exempt as selectable cards. The amount field only appears when it makes sense.
Per line item or per invoice — A single toggle decides whether tax is calculated on every line item or once on the invoice total.
Apply tax rates — A clear "Services" parent card groups Memberships, Credit packages, and Appointments, with a separate row for Gift cards and Pay rate. Each row can override the default.
Inheritance banner — A line at the top of Services reads the live VAT percentage so the owner sees at a glance what every category inherits.

2 Referral
The settings screen where the owner runs the rewards-based referral program. Still being polished — will appear in the next demo push.
Capabilities:
Master switch — A single toggle turns the entire referral program on or off, with a confirmation before the flip.
Reward rules and limits — Side-panel editor that sets who the referrer earns, who the friend earns, the unlock trigger (sign up / first purchase / first class), the per-member referral cap, expiry days, and monthly program budget.
Eligibility and fraud controls — Side-panel editor with four guard rails: prevent self-referral, new customers only, require minimum first spend, and credits redeemable across all branches.
Customize referral information — Full-page editor with a title field, click-to-insert variables ({{referrer}} / {{friend}} / {{trigger}} / {{cap}}), a rich text editor, and a live preview of exactly what the customer will see.
Customer detail Referrals tab — Three summary cards (referral code, total referrals out of the cap, total bonus credits earned), plus a sortable table with a new Expiry date column so the owner can see when each referred reward runs out.

3 Business & Locations
The settings screen where the owner manages the studio's legal identity, branches, and rooms. Now live in this demo.
Capabilities:
Legal identity fields — Trading name, legal entity, tax number, billing address, and similar fields captured once at the studio level.
Branch location scope — Each branch can be tagged as a club, a recovery spa, or both, so categories and services appear at the right location.
Branch list and detail — Add, edit, deactivate, or archive any branch. Rooms can be added or edited per branch with their own opening hours.
Forma Spa wired in — The recovery branch has full opening hours and shows up consistently across the schedule, services, and customer search.

4 Branding
The settings screen where the owner customizes the studio's customer-facing look. Still being polished — will appear in the next demo push.
Capabilities:
Three-step Customize design — Step through brand colors, typeface, and app icons. Each step shows a fitted phone preview that updates as the owner edits.
Six fonts to choose from — Pick the typeface that fits the brand; the preview switches font live.
Login, Home, and Class previews — Polished previews so the owner can confirm the brand reads correctly on the screens customers see first.
Portal preferences sub-page — Toggles for the customer experience (light / dark default, social handles, support email).

5 Customer Products catalog
The customer-facing catalog of memberships, packages, and gift cards. Now live in this demo.
Capabilities:
One catalog, three tabs — Memberships, Packages, and Gift cards in a single browseable surface.
Checkout flow — Pick a product, apply a promo code if needed, go through processing, and land on a confirmation screen.
Gift card design picker — Customers can choose the look of the gift card before buying.
Live with the admin catalog — Anything the owner adds in the admin Products module appears here right away.

6 Schedule — Day view Spa column
A polish for the admin Day view so spa appointments don't get lost. Now live in this demo.
Capabilities:
Recovery column — Spa appointments (sauna, breathwork, massage, IV therapy) now render in their own "Recovery" column at the right of the instructor lanes, instead of being hidden in Day view.
Cross-view consistency — Week, Month, and List views already rendered these correctly; Day view now matches.

General updates
Admin Integrations — Payments and Apps are now reachable from the Settings landing as a single page with two tabs. Cards, Cash, and Bank transfer ship enabled by default so POS and customer checkout have working payment options out of the box.
Schedule Spa rules — The class schedule create form now hides the Spa branch (services-only); the service create form hides the club branches when the service is non-recovery, so the wrong branch can never be picked by accident.
Settings landing — Single 4-card layout (Studio / Operations / Customer / Platform). Each card links straight to its sub-module, no more long-list scrolling.
Persistent demo state — Anything created, edited, cancelled, or marked present during a demo session survives a page refresh and a tab close. Reset by clearing the site data.
